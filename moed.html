Langages : HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, Ajax, MYSQL And more


- This is a project that is an internal website of the Syrian Ministry of Education, where teachers are evaluated in Syrian schools all over Syria.

- We have several actors in this system :
1- SuperAdmin : Admin CRUD , SuperGuide CRUD , Guide CRUD , Teacher CRUD And edit his profile information.

2- Admin : SuperGuide CRUD , View the evaluations of teachers , Print the evaluations And edit his profile information.

3- SuperGuide : Guide CRUD , View the evaluations of teachers depending on the subject of the superguide , Print the evaluations , Add note to the evaluations And edit his profile information.

4- Guide : Teachers CRUD , Add A New evaluations for teacher And edit his profile information.

- Important information is encrypted such as username and password and more.

- Entry information verification system.
